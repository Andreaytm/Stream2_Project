School Donations Data Dashboard

The School Donations Data Dashboard displays statistics of donations given per school state. 7 States were chosen at random to show the current education improvements required in the state of Delaware. These include: Alaska (AK), Delaware (DE), Hawaii (HI), Idaho (ID), Minnesota (MN), Kansas (KS), and Kentucky (KY).

Graphs & Charts

The data displayed includes: charts, graphs and tables of:

  • Total number of donations given
  • Total value of donations given in US Dollars
  • Poverty Levels
  • Funding Status
  • Number of donations made over the years
  • Type of resources donated
  • 'Teach for America' teacher availability
  • School grade levels of students
  • Primary focus areas, and
  • Primary focus subjects
