Our Campaign

Our Impact

The funding provided to DonorsChoose is used to support teachers and students. From resources such as pens, books, money for school trips, printers, computers and much more. The money donated not only provides resources but also confidence self worth and pride. It is an investment in our future and in our kids lives.

Our Aim


DonorsChoose has supported a lot of schools in various US states. Our aim is to increase our reach, so that no student and teacher is disadvantaged from their location. We hope to have equal education for everyone. That each student and teacher is provided with what they need to study, to learn and to teach.
